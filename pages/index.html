How this works?

Your password is hashed after you enter it. The hashed password is sent to my server, where an algorithm checks if the rockyou.txt wordlist contains it. If your password is not in the wordlist, you are safe. If it is, you should change your password.

What is rockyou.txt?

Rockyou wordlist is a password dictionary used to help to perform different types of password cracking attacks. It is the collection of the most used and potential passwords.
